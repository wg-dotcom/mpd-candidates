About the Role

A hands-on operator to own the full demand generation engine

MPD Pool Holdings is a Chicago-based private equity firm focused on acquiring and consolidating swimming pool design, construction, and maintenance businesses across the United States. The company operates a portfolio of pool service brands with a buy-and-build strategy, targeting established family-owned and regional pool companies to create a national platform. They value speed, ownership, accountability, and data-driven decision making.

Mychal is looking for a Lead Generation Manager who can step in and immediately take ownership of demand generation across multiple brands and locations. This is not a strategy-only role. The person needs to execute campaigns, manage paid media budgets, optimize funnels, and build live marketing dashboards that connect ad spend directly to booked jobs inside ServiceTitan. The core metrics are CPL, cost per booked job, and marketing-sourced revenue.

The first batch of candidates was rejected because the member wants someone with a deeper, more hands-on performance marketing background. We sourced three new candidates, each with strong paid media execution experience, funnel optimization skills, and the technical chops to build attribution systems and dashboards. All three have direct experience managing Google Ads and Meta campaigns at scale with measurable ROI.
